Overview

Automobili Lamborghini S.p.A. is an Italian manufacturer of luxury sports cars and SUVs located in Sant’Agata Bolognese. Founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini, the company initially focused on producing grand touring cars to compete with established marques like Ferrari.

Lamborghini gained fame for its powerful engines, sleek designs, and technological innovation. Some of its most notable models include the Miura, Countach, Diablo, Murciélago, Gallardo, and Aventador. These cars are characterized by their distinctive styling, aggressive performance, and often feature exotic materials in their construction.

Despite changes in ownership, Lamborghini has maintained its reputation for producing some of the most desirable and exclusive sports cars in the world. The company continues to push boundaries in terms of performance, design, and technology, cementing its status as a symbol of luxury and automotive excellence.

Ferruccio Lamborghini

Ferruccio Lamborghini was an Italian entrepreneur and the founder of the renowned luxury sports car manufacturer, Lamborghini. Born on April 28, 1916, in Cento, Italy, Lamborghini initially pursued a career in mechanics and engineering, establishing a successful tractor manufacturing business, Lamborghini Trattori, after World War II.

Before the business

After the war, Lamborghini founded Lamborghini Trattori in 1948, specializing in manufacturing high-quality tractors using surplus military hardware. His commitment to innovation and quality quickly propelled his tractors to prominence in the agricultural industry, establishing him as a successful businessman.

Beyond tractors, Lamborghini diversified his business ventures into other fields, including heating and air conditioning systems, with companies like Lamborghini Bruciatori and Lamborghini Calor. Despite his success in these sectors, Lamborghini harbored a deep passion for luxury cars. He owned several high-performance vehicles, including Ferraris, Maseratis, and Alfa Romeos.

Creation of the company

Later, Lamborghini turned his attention to his passion for high-performance cars and established Automobili Lamborghini in 1963. Dissatisfied with the service he received from Ferrari, Lamborghini aimed to create superior grand touring cars that rivaled Ferrari’s offerings in terms of performance and luxury.

After selling Lamborghini in the 1970s, Ferruccio Lamborghini retired to pursue other interests, including winemaking and property development. He passed away on February 20, 1993, leaving behind a lasting legacy as an automotive visionary and entrepreneur whose name remains synonymous with luxury and innovation in the world of high-performance automobiles.

Current lineup

The current Lamborghini lineup consists of the revolutionary Revuelto, which is the first HPEV (High Performance Electrified Vehicle) created by Lamborghini. There is also the iconic Huracán, equipped with a powerful V10 engine and cutting-edge technologies for outstanding performance. Additionally, the Lamborghini Urus stands as the world’s first Super Sport Utility Vehicle, combining luxury, sportiness, and performance with everyday comfort and versatility. The lineup also includes Limited Series models for exclusive clients and visionary Concept models that push the boundaries of technology and design.

Sports cars

Revuelto

The Lamborghini Revuelto is a mid-engine plug-in hybrid sports car introduced as the successor to the Aventador. The car is named after a Spanish fighting bull and is the first Lamborghini to feature a true hybrid drivetrain. It boasts new design elements such as Y-shaped LED lights, an active aero spoiler, and an exposed engine bay. The interior has been enhanced for increased comfort, and a new hybrid driving mode called “Città” provides reduced power for urban driving. The car’s construction includes a carbon fiber monocoque, making it lighter and more rigid than its predecessor.

Huracán

The Lamborghini Huracán is a high-performance sports car equipped with a powerful V10 engine and the latest technologies for optimal performance. It is designed to offer maximum control under various driving conditions, with features like the Lamborghini Doppia Frizione (LDF) dual clutch gearbox and electronically-controlled four-wheel drive. The Huracán promises an exhilarating driving experience, showcasing a perfect balance of performance, control, and innovation.

SUVs

Urus

The Lamborghini Urus is the world’s first Super Sport Utility Vehicle that combines luxury, sportiness, and performance with comfort and versatility. It offers best-in-class driving dynamics, unmistakable design elegance, and suitability for everyday driving in various environments. The Urus has a distinct engine sound and high performance, making it unique and far from typical in the SUV market.
