Ranch Roasted Red Potatos

Total time: 30 mins      Serves: Four

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs red potatos
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp Ranch dry seasoning mix
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  2. In a large bowl add potatoes and toss with olive oil. Then, add in the Ranch dressing mix and salt and pepper to taste. Toss well to coat each potato piece evenly.
  3. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Pour potatoes onto the baking sheet and spread out potatoes evenly in a single layer.
  4. Bake potatoes in oven until potatoes are cooked through, and edges start to turn very dark brown, approximately 20-25 minutes. To check to see if they are done, pierce one of the larger pieces of potato with a fork to see if it is soft.
  5. Serve immediately.


Air Fryer Bacon

Total time: 12 mins      Serves: 3-6

Ingredients

  • 6 streaky bacon rashers or 3 back bacon rasher

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Lay the bacon in a single layer in the base of an air fryer – be sure the layers aren't overlapping. The size of the air fryer basket will determine how many rashers you can cook at one time.
  3. Set the air fryer to 200C and cook streaky bacon rashers for 6-10 mins – just-cooked bacon will take 6 mins, but crispier bacon will take 9-10 mins. Cook back bacon rashers for 4-8 mins, depending on your preference. If you are cooking in batches, remember that the second batch will usually be quicker as the air fryer has heated up.
  4. Remove the bacon using tongs and put on kitchen paper to drain before serving.


Rolo Pretzel Bites

Total time: 20 mins      Servings: 36

Ingredients

  • Bag of mini pretzels
  • Bag of Rolos
  • Pecan Halves
  • M&Ms

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Place pretzels on an ungreased sheet pan.
  3. Place unwrapped Rolos in a single layer on top of each pretzel and bake for 3-5 minutes. You don't want them too melted.
  4. Remove from oven and push pecan half (or candy) halfway down on top of each Rolo.
  5. Store leftovers in an airtight container.
